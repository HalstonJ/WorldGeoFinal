Source One: The Bengal Famine

The Bengal famine was one of the many mass starvations in history, taking place in British India as terrible Colonial Policies restricted food and crops from many individuals within. Tarakchandra Das documented his experiences with locals during this period, interviewing and documenting the lives of the famine's victims. Das highlights the mass migration to urban centers across countries due to the conditions laid out in the colony. Das's studies on this famine were some of the first systematic field studies on the topic of famine, bringing awareness to hopefully lead to its end.
